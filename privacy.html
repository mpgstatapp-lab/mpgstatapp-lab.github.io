Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MPG Stat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

MPG Stat is a fuel economy tracking app. We built it to be simple, private, and useful. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data collection

We collect nothing

MPG Stat does not collect any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no login, no analytics, and no advertising.

All data you enter — fillup records, odometer readings, and calculated statistics — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third parties

No third-party sharing

MPG Stat does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks included in the app.

Data storage

Your data, your device

All fillup history and statistics are stored locally on your iPhone. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. We have no access to your data and cannot recover it on your behalf.

Children

Children's privacy

MPG Stat does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, this is straightforward.

Changes

Policy updates

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Any future changes will be reflected here.
